Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BharatScript

What scripts does BharatScript support?

We support all major Indian scripts including Devanagari, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Gujarati, Gurmukhi, and Odia.

Is BharatScript free to use?

Yes, BharatScript is completely free to use for all your transliteration needs.

How accurate is the transliteration?

Our transliteration is highly accurate, using advanced algorithms and the Sanscript library for precise conversions between scripts.

Can I use BharatScript for commercial purposes?

Yes, BharatScript can be used for both personal and commercial purposes. Please check our terms of service for more details.
